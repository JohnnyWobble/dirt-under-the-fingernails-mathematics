Sample Problems

These are some sample problems we went over and solving during class this year. For the most part, these are problems that can be visualized well, however, they do range in difficulty and methodology.

Sample Problem 1

Given

Consider the following model, consisting of propagation functions:

`I(x,y,t+1) = U(x,y,t) * [0.15(I(x-1,y,t) + I(x,y-1,t) + I(x+1,y,t) + I(x,y+1,t)) + 0.4I(x,y,t)]`

`U(x,y,t+1) = U(x,y,t) - I(x,y,t)`

`R(x,y,t+1) = R(x,y,t) + I(x,y,t)`

for `1 <= x <=5`, `1 <= y <=5`, and `t >= 0`

Initial conditions:

`I(x,y,0)={(.1, "if " x=2 " and " y=3), (0, "else"):}`

`U(x,y,0)={(.9, "if " x=2 " and " y=3), (1, "else"):}`

`R(x,y,0)=0`

Questions

1)

Compute `I`, `U`, and `R` for all values of `x` and `y` between `1` and `5`, and for all small values of `t`.

2)

What do you suppose is being modeled here? How would you improve the model?

Take the class and find out!
